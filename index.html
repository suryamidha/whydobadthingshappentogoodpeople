Why do bad things happen to good people?

an unfiltered list of answers from some really smart people

What is this?

This is a pleasure project by Surya Midha born out of a bout of teenage existentialism that was quickly resolved with some Wes Anderson. I know very little about anything, so I wanted to learn from people who know a lot about everything. Academics all over the United States were emailed a simple question: why do bad things happen to good people? We limited the response to 500 words or less.

My Promise

This site is only curated insofar as the responses are formatted in an aesthetically pleasing way, but the content has not been edited or altered in any significant way.

People usually put copyright down here, but I have no idea how to do any of that. Thanks for reading all the way until here, I guess.
